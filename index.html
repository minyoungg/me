Hello.

My name is Minyoung Huh (Jacob) a PhD student working on machine learning and computer vision at MIT CSAIL / Embodied Intelligence working with Phillip Isola and Pulkit Agrawal . I received my Bachelors from UC Berkeley advised under Alexei (Alyosha) Efros at Berkeley AI Research (BAIR). Prior to joining BAIR, I worked with Maysam Chamanzar and Michel Maharbiz at Swarm Lab.

My current research interest is in perception through time, emergence of behaviors + representation, and generative models. I am also interested in image synthesis and theory of deep learning.
